Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure and private?

Yes. 1on1 uses industry-standard AES-256 encryption. Meeting notes are restricted only to the participants unless explicitly shared with HR/Leadership.

Can we import our existing templates?

Absolutely. Our editor allows you to paste your current markdown or rich text templates and convert them into interactive 1on1 sessions instantly.

How does the AI sentiment analysis work?

Our AI processes anonymized transcriptions to identify patterns of sentiment over time. It helps managers spot "drift" in employee engagement before it becomes a problem.
